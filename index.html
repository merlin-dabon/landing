Quick description

PNL (short for Peace N' Lovés) is a French rap group composed of two brothers: Ademo and N.O.S, whose real names are Tarik and Nabil Andrieu. The group is characterized by a total absence of interviews in the press2, as well as by innovative film clips in France3, shot all over the world. They have released 4 rap albums "Que la famille", "Le monde chico", "Dans la légende", "Deux frères". You can see below 3 of the best songs of Pnl.
